Hangzhou

West Lake (Xī Hú) in Hangzhou is a stunning freshwater lake renowned for its picturesque beauty, tranquil landscapes, and historical significance. Surrounded by lush hills, ancient temples, and traditional pavilions, it has inspired poets, artists, and philosophers for centuries.

chengdu

Chengdu's Giant Panda Breeding Research Base is a world-renowned sanctuary dedicated to the conservation and breeding of the endangered giant panda. Visitors can observe these adorable creatures in naturalistic enclosures while learning about ongoing efforts to protect their species and habitat.
